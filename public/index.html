About Ammayi Service

Ammayi Service is dedicated to helping women who have not had relationships, providing them with guidance and support. Managed by Shahid, our platform focuses on empowering women and ensuring their well-being.

Our mission is to uplift women by offering emotional support, career opportunities, mentorship, and financial aid. We believe in fostering confidence and independence for every woman.
